Why is Web Security so Important to Know?

As technology changes, it's getting harder for businesses and customers to keep their personal and professional information on the web secure. Web security protects you by keeping hackers from accessing personal and sensitive information. If a hacker is successful to get into your information , they can spread viruses from computer to computer, making it difficult to find the origin of the problem.

A list to avoid the risk of security attacks:

  • Use Strong Passwords
  • Two-Factor Authorization
  • Always Use Secure Networks
  • Use More Than One Email Address
  • Identifying Secure Websites
  • Get a VPN
